===========
Intro
===========

This is meant to be a comprehensive guide to timing a race at Mammoth Mountain, as well as using and maintaining our existing infrastructure. The focus is on FIS and US Ski and Snowboard scored races, and rule numbers will be referenced where applicable. The pages in this section will describe the basic goals and concepts behind timing a ski race.
